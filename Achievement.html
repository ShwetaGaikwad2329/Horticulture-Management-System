Indian Bonsai Association

Indian Bonsai Association (Delhi) was founded on March 9, 1981 by late Dr Leila Dhanda and Dr Hussain Tayebbhoy to propagate the art of Bonsai. It has grown into one of the leading organization of the country with around 200 active members.
  • Dr. Leila Dhanda
  • Founder of the Association.
  • Played a key role in promoting Bonsai Culture in India by teaching and guiding the bonsai enthusiasts.
  • She was the nucleus of the association
  • Dr. Hussain Tayebbhoy
  • Founder President of the Association.
  • He set the Bonsai culture in India on a firm footing by his active participation and guidance.
  • He was very popular among bonsai lovers.
